Frequently asked questions

How do I reset my password?

Go to login page and click "Forgot password". You'll receive an email with instructions.

Can I use AGH without internet?

Yes, basic farm tools work offline. Data syncs when you're back online.

How do I add members to my cooperative?

In the cooperative dashboard, click "Manage members" and send invites by email.
