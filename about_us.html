Global Reach

Over 1 million farmers across five continents trust AGH to improve yields and well‑being.

Our story

Founded in 2018 by agronomists and software engineers, AGH started as a small project in the Niger Delta. We saw that farmers had incredible intuition but lacked real‑time data. Today we combine satellite imaging, IoT sensors, and meditation tools to care for both the land and the farmer.
